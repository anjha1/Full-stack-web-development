Digital marketing

Digital marketing is the promotion of products and services to consumers using digital channels and electronic technologies. It can also be called online marketing. What is Digital Marketing? - Mailchimp Digital marketing, also called online marketing, is the promotion of brands to connect with potential customers using the internet and other forms of digital communication.
